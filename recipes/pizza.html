Pizza Recipe

Writer: Daddy Kim Jong Un

Materials

Pizza Dough

Tomato Sauce

Cheese

Description

Pizza is a beloved dish that originated in Italy and has gained worldwide popularity. It typically consists of a round, flattened dough base topped with a savory sauce, cheese, and a variety of ingredients. Baked in an oven until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted and bubbly, pizza offers a delightful combination of flavors, textures, and aromas that make it a universally loved comfort food.

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough: In a mixing bowl, combine flour, water, yeast, salt, and any other desired ingredients. Knead the dough until it becomes smooth and elastic. Allow it to rise in a warm place for about an hour or until it doubles in size.
  2. Preheat the oven: Set your oven to the appropriate temperature (usually around 475-500°F or 245-260°C) and preheat it while you prepare the toppings.
  3. Roll out the dough: On a floured surface, roll out the dough to your desired thickness and shape. Transfer it to a baking sheet or pizza stone that has been lightly greased or lined with parchment paper.
  4. Add the sauce and toppings: Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle shredded cheese over the sauce, and then add your desired toppings such as vegetables, meats, and herbs.
  5. Bake the pizza: Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for about 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  6. Remove from the oven and serve: Carefully remove the pizza from the oven using oven mitts or a pizza peel. Allow it to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
